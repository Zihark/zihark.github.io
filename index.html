Welcome!

Hello and welcome to my projects page!
My name is Mario Moreno, and I am a Front-end developer from Santiago, Chile.

Who am I?

I'm a Front-end developer, based in the city of Santiago, Chile, with more than ten years of experience overall in Web Design and Development, both as a freelance and a hired professional.

I graduated from Electronics Engineering back in 2011, but got more into Software Engineering right after my first job in Electronics, mainly because I really liked coding way before I began working as a paid engineer.

Personal profile

I was born and raised in Santiago, Chile, so I'm fluent in Chilean spanish, which is one of the most interesting spanish accents worldwide. I started learning english back when I was 13 years old, so I'm confident speaking both english and spanish, and who knows what new language I might add in the near future!

I'm no stranger to the challenges of career and mindset shifting, so I like learning new stuff a lot, and I like teaching the stuff I learn even more!
One of my biggest motivations is to bring technology to Everyone, no distinctions, and I consider myself to be an inclusion advocate.
